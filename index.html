The SKELLiES

4 piece live band

The Skellies are expert party instigators, specialising in timeless Rock N Roll and Indie Classics spanning the past four decades.

Over the past 15 years, we've consistently ignited dance floors from beginning to end with our electrifying sets. Unlike typical bands with two 45-minute performances, we kick off the celebration early and keep the party alive all night long.

For us, every performance is an opportunity to deliver the biggest hits, create incredible vibes, and craft enduring memories. Whether it's a wedding, a lively party, a charitable event, or just a reason to celebrate, reach out to us, and we'll ensure you have an unforgettable night.
